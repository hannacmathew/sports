WELCOME

TO MOTORCYCLE RACING CLUB

Our Motorcycle Racing Club is the world's oldest motorcycle racing club and one of the largest ones. Founded in 1909, it is now located at offices in Chicago, Illinois. This heritage is significant as the club enjoys a position at the heart of two- and three-wheeled racing in the US, and many stars of past and present started with this club.
